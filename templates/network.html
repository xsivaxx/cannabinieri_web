Kaos Cube

Anonymous Decentralized Network Sharing

The Kaos Cube is a groundbreaking, decentralized Wi-Fi router transforming personal internet infrastructure. Leveraging Ethereum-based digital tokens, it offers secure, private networking and bandwidth sharing, controlled via Ether tokens. With Cjdns encryption and onion routing, akin to Tor's principles, it ensures user anonymity and data security. The Kaos Cube is a key component for the innovative Spider automation system, marking a stride towards a user-governed internet ecosystem.
